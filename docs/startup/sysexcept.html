50_sysexception module¶

Give a detailed, colored traceback and drop into pdb on exceptions.

exception profile_default.startup.50_sysexception.ExceptionHook[source][source]¶

Bases: BaseException

Custom exception hook for IPython.

From the IPython official documentation:

Print out a formatted exception traceback.

Optional arguments:

  • out: an open file-like object to direct output to.

  • tb_offset: the number of frames to skip over in the stack, on a per-call basis (this overrides temporarily the instance’s tb_offset given at initialization time.
